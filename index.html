About FireBird Hacks

FireBird Hacks is Arcadia’s first-ever hackathon, open to students and young innovators in our community. It’s an action-packed 12-hour event of learning, building, and having fun with no prior experience required! A hackathon is an event where people come together to create projects in a short amount of time, and despite the name, it doesn’t involve hacking into anything! Over the course of the day, participants design and build original projects from scratch, learning new technical skills while putting their creativity to the test. Whether you’re a beginner or an experienced coder, you’ll have access to mentors and resources to help bring your ideas to life. Participants can join individually or in teams, encouraging collaboration, problem-solving, and peer learning.

At the end of the event, participants pitch their projects to judges, with prizes awarded for the most functional, creative, and user-friendly designs. Beyond the competition, FireBird Hacks is a chance to network with peers, share ideas, gain practical experience, and showcase your skills in a supportive and inspiring environment. We hope you’ll join us for this historic day of learning, building, and fun!
